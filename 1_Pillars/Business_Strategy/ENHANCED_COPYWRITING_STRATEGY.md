## 🎯 A/B Testing Framework

### Copy Elements to Test
1. **Headlines**
   - "Sua jornada internacional" vs "Sua carreira global"
   - "Calculada com precisão" vs "Simplificada com tecnologia"

2. **CTAs**
   - "Começar Agora" vs "Criar Conta Grátis"
   - "Explorar Marketplace" vs "Encontrar Profissionais"

3. **Value Props**
   - Focus on "segurança" vs "rapidez" vs "economia"

4. **Social Proof**
   - Numbers focus ("2.400+ jornadas") vs emotional focus ("Histórias reais")

### Testing Priorities
1. **Hero Section** (highest impact)
2. **Pricing CTA** (conversion critical)
3. **Marketplace CTA** (revenue driver)
4. **Social Proof** (trust building)
