## 📝 Content Guidelines (No Fake Data)

### What TO Include
✅ **Real Features**:
- Diagnostic tool (if it exists)
- Career planning features (actual functionality)
- Document templates (if available)
- Blog content (real articles)

✅ **Honest Messaging**:
- "We're building a marketplace" (not "126 professionals")
- "Join our waitlist" (not "Browse providers")
- "Coming soon" (for features in development)
- "Beta" or "Early Access" (for new features)

✅ **Real Social Proof**:
- Actual user testimonials (with permission)
- Real company partnerships (if any)
- Genuine press mentions (if any)
- Verifiable statistics (from real data)

### What NOT to Include
❌ **Fake Data**:
- Made-up provider numbers
- Fictional testimonials
- Fake user statistics
- Placeholder ratings/reviews

❌ **Unverifiable Claims**:
- "Thousands of users" (unless true)
- "Best in market" (unless proven)
- "Guaranteed results" (legally risky)
- Specific success rates (without data)

❌ **Misleading Information**:
- Features that don't exist yet
- Services not actually offered
- Partnerships that aren't real
- Fake urgency or scarcity

## 📱 Mobile-First Implementation

### Mobile Priorities
1. **Fast Loading**: <3s on 3G
2. **Touch-Friendly**: 44px minimum tap targets
3. **Readable**: 16px minimum font size
4. **Simple Navigation**: Hamburger menu
5. **Forms**: Easy to fill on mobile

### Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile**: 320px - 768px (primary focus)
- **Tablet**: 768px - 1024px
- **Desktop**: 1024px+

### Performance Targets
- **Lighthouse Score**: 90+ on mobile
- **First Contentful Paint**: <1.5s
- **Time to Interactive**: <3s
- **Core Web Vitals**: All green
