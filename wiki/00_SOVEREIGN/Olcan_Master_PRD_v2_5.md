## IV. Mathematical Engine & Forensic Logic

This section provides the explicit mathematical foundations of the Olcan Career Operating System. All logic is implemented in the [economics.py](file:///Users/ciromoraes/Documents/THE-Code-Base/olcan-compass/apps/api-core-v2.5/app/schemas/economics.py) and [psychology.py](file:///Users/ciromoraes/Documents/THE-Code-Base/olcan-compass/apps/api-core-v2.5/app/schemas/psychology.py) schemas.

### 1. Formula for Opportunity Cost ({ia}$)

### 1. Formula for Opportunity Cost ($C_{ia}$)
The weight of inaction is quantified per unit of time (Day) to induce a "Healthy Urgency" in the user.

$$C_{ia} = \frac{(S_{target} - S_{current})}{365}$$

Where:
- $S_{target}$: The target annual salary in the global market.
- $S_{current}$: The user's current local annual salary (converted to target currency).
- **Consolidated Logic**: The system displays the accumulated cost since the diagnostic date to visually demonstrate the price of stagnation.

### 2. Logic for Temporal Match Score ($M_t$)
Matches a user's temporal preference (willingness to move soon vs. later) with the expected duration of a recruitment route.

$$M_t = \max(0, 100 - (\Delta_{temp} \times 2))$$

Where:
- $\Delta_{temp}$: The absolute difference (in months) between user preference and route template duration.
- **Result**: A 0-100 score where 100 is a perfect temporal fit.

### 3. Performance-Bound Escrow Logic
The system enforces the "Clinical Quality" of the marketplace by delaying fund release to providers (Mentors, Agencies).

- **Condition**: `Release` if `Improvement_Achieved` $\geq 10\%$.
- **Improvement Metric**: A delta calculation between `readiness_score_t0` (pre-service) and `readiness_score_t1` (post-service).
- **Resolution**: If the threshold is not met, the `Funds_held` are automatically flagged for **Resolution Audit** by the Nexus Finance Tracker agent.

### 4. Stall Detection & Churn Prediction
Predicts abandonment based on the relationship between user types and route friction.

- **Variables**: `risk_level` (Boolean), `temporal_mismatch` (Float).
- **Momentum Decay**: If activity drops (logins, task completion), the `Momentum_score` degrades by a factor of 0.8 per period of inactivity.
- **Intervention**: Triggered at `momentum < 20`.
