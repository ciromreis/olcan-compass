# Shared Systems: App + Marketing Site

## Purpose

Define what should be shared between:
- `apps/app-compass-v2`
- `apps/site-marketing-v2.5`

so both surfaces operationalize the same product vision.

---

## Shared Product Primitives

## 1. Archetypes
Shared across:
- onboarding
- diagnosis results
- companion identity
- marketing segmentation
- landing page personalization

## 2. Companion states
Shared across:
- hero visuals
- product preview flows
- in-app progression
- lifecycle messaging

## 3. Product taxonomy
Shared across:
- plans
- modules
- benefits
- route categories
- service categories

## 4. Trust language
Shared across:
- verification badges
- provider credibility
- escrow messaging
- claims around safety and confidence

## 5. Event schema
Shared across:
- analytics
- lifecycle automation
- marketing retargeting
- gamification triggers

---

## Recommended Shared Packages

```text
packages/
  ui-components/
  product-taxonomy/
  design-tokens/
  event-schema/
```

---

## Immediate Opportunities

### Marketing site can borrow from app
- archetype language
- companion visual states
- milestone metaphors
- route/progress narrative
- trust system visuals

### App can borrow from marketing site
- stronger emotional storytelling
- clearer product packaging
- cleaner benefit framing
- more premium visual hierarchy
- liquid-glass restraint and clarity

---

## Rule
The site should promise what the app can operationalize.
The app should deliver the emotional language the site introduces.
