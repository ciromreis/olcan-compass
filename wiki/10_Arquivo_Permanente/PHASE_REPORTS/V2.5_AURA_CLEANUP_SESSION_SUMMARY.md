## Þÿ¡ **How This Relates to Olcan Compass**

### **The Big Picture**

**Olcan Compass** is a lifecycle-driven mobility platform for graduate school applicants. The product has evolved through multiple versions:

1. **V1** - Initial MVP (stable, deployed)
2. **V2** - Production version (stable, deployed on Netlify)
3. **V2.5** - Current development version with refined UX

### **The "Aura" Concept**

The **Aura** (formerly "Companion") is Olcan's **gamification layer** that:
- Represents the user's **operational presence** in the platform
- Evolves based on **real product engagement** (not arbitrary game mechanics)
- Provides **motivational feedback** through:
  - Care activities (daily engagement)
  - Evolution stages (milestone progression)
  - Achievements (meaningful accomplishments)
  - Streaks (consistency tracking)

### **Why This Cleanup Matters**

1. **Product Clarity**
   - "Companion" suggested a pet/game mechanic (not the intent)
   - "Aura" better represents the user's **operational energy** and **presence**
   - Aligns with Olcan's philosophy of **meaningful engagement over entertainment**

2. **Technical Debt Reduction**
   - V2.5 inherited experimental code from earlier iterations
   - Mixed terminology created confusion in the codebase
   - This cleanup creates a **clean foundation** for future development

3. **Architectural Alignment**
   - Gamification should **reinforce product value**, not distract from it
   - XP comes from **real actions** (completing documents, booking sessions)
   - Achievements celebrate **real milestones** (first application, interview success)
