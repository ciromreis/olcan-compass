## 💡 Key Insights

### Design Philosophy:
The "liquid-glass metamodern" aesthetic is about:
- **Depth through layers** (not flat design)
- **Transparency and blur** (frosted glass effects)
- **Metamodern oscillation** (playful yet professional)
- **Game-like interactions** (satisfying, responsive)

### Technical Approach:
- **Custom components** (not off-the-shelf libraries)
- **Built on Radix UI primitives** (accessible foundations)
- **Styled with Tailwind + custom utilities** (glass effects)
- **Animated with Framer Motion** (spring physics)
- **Fully typed with TypeScript** (strict mode)

### Quality Standards:
- **Accessibility first** (WCAG AA minimum)
- **Performance optimized** (60fps desktop, 30fps mobile)
- **Fully documented** (Storybook + written docs)
- **Thoroughly tested** (unit + visual + accessibility)
