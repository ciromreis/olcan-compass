2. Use `render.yaml` configuration
3. Configure environment variables:
   - `DATABASE_URL`: Production PostgreSQL

   - `SMTP_HOST`: Email service configuration

### **Step 2: Configure Email Service**
```bash

SMTP_PORT=587
SMTP_USERNAME=your-email@gmail.com

SMTP_USE_TLS=true
```
